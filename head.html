About Us

Greatwellness Health Care Services provides compassionate and reliable home care and healthcare staffing services. Our dedicated team of professionals supports individuals and families by delivering personalized care designed to promote dignity, independence, and well-being.

We are committed to improving quality of life through professional, respectful, and client-focused support services.

Our Services

Personal Support Worker (PSW)

Assistance with daily living activities such as personal hygiene, mobility, meal preparation, and companionship.

Developmental Support Worker (DSW)

Support for individuals with developmental disabilities to help build independence and life skills.

Home Care Services

Professional in-home care that allows individuals to live safely and comfortably in their own homes.

Nursing Services

Qualified nurses providing health monitoring, medication support, and post-hospital care.

Companionship

Emotional support and companionship to help reduce isolation and improve well-being.

Healthcare Staffing

Reliable healthcare professionals available for hospitals, clinics, and care facilities.

Our Approach

Person-Centered Care

At Greatwellness Health Care Services, we follow a Person-Centered Approach. This means every individual receives care that respects their personal preferences, goals, and independence.

Our caregivers work closely with clients and families to develop customized care plans that promote dignity, safety, and well-being.

  • Respect for independence and dignity
  • Individualized care plans
  • Compassionate and trained caregivers
  • Focus on quality of life and well-being
